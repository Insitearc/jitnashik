"The Artificial Intelligence and Data Science department is a hub for innovation, where we bridge the gap between complex algorithms and real-world solutions."

Our Artificial Intelligence and Data Science program is designed for the modern era, focusing on the development of intelligent systems and the analysis of vast datasets. Students gain expertise in machine learning, neural networks, and big data architecture.

Through cutting-edge labs and industry partnerships, we prepare our students to become the architects of the future, capable of driving AI-driven transformations across various sectors.

Vision

To develop a world-class center of excellence in AI and Data Science that creates talented professionals capable of making significant global impacts through technological innovation.

Mission

  • To offer a industry-relevant curriculum that combines academic rigor with practical exposure to emergent AI technologies.
  • To foster an environment of continuous learning and research in data science and intelligent systems.
  • To build strong bridges with global AI research institutes and industry pioneers.
