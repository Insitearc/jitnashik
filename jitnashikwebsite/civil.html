"Civil engineering is the heritage of building societies. At JIT, we train our students to build a sustainable and resilient future."

The Department of Civil Engineering at JIT Nashik offers a comprehensive curriculum that covers structural design, transportation engineering, and geotechnical studies. We emphasize practical learning through survey camps and site visits to major infrastructure projects.

Our graduates are equipped with state-of-the-art tools like AutoCAD, Revit, and STAAD Pro, preparing them for careers in infrastructure development, urban planning, and environmental engineering.

Vision

To develop the department as a center of excellence that produces globally competent civil engineers with a sense of social and environmental responsibility.

Mission

  • To provide modern teaching-learning environment for high-quality technical education in civil engineering.
  • To instill professional ethics, leadership qualities and environmental awareness among students.
  • To create opportunities for industry interaction and research to serve society.
