Foundation of Technical Prowess

Jawahar Education Society (JES) was founded with the singular vision of providing high-quality education to the youth of rural and urban Maharashtra. Under the visionary leadership of our founders, the society has grown from a modest beginning to a sprawling academic conglomerate.

The society's flagship institution, Jawahar Institute of Technology, Nashik, exemplifies our commitment to engineering excellence. We believe that education is the most powerful tool for social transformation. By integrating traditional academic values with modern technological advancements, we ensure our students are not just degree holders but competent innovators.
