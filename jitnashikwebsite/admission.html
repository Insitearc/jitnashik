Admission Process

1
Online Inquiry

Fill the inquiry form on our website to register your interest and get counseling.

2
Registration

Official registration through the DTE Maharashtra CAP portal is mandatory for all applicants.

3
Document Verification

Visit our Facilitation Center (FC) for physical verification of your original documents.

4
Seat Allotment

Confirm your allotted seat by reporting to the institute and submitting fees.

Eligibility Criteria

Category Requirements
Open Category 45% marks in PCM in Class 12th + Valid CET/JEE Score
Reserved (MH Only) 40% marks in PCM in Class 12th + Valid CET/JEE Score
Diploma Holders 45% marks in Diploma Engineering (40% for Reserved)
