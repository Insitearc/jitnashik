"Our mission is to empower students with foundational and advanced knowledge in computer science, preparing them to innovate and lead in a rapidly evolving digital landscape."

The Department of Computer Engineering at JIT Nashik is dedicated to providing an education that combines rigorous academic study with the excitement of discovery. Since its inception in 2008, the department has grown into a center of excellence, equipped with 10+ modern laboratories and led by a distinguished faculty.

To produce globally competent and socially responsible computer engineers by fostering innovation and research-oriented environment to meet the challenges of the ever-changing IT industry.

Departmental Facilities

AI & Robotics Lab

Equipped with NVIDIA RTX workstations and robotic kits for hands-on experimentation in machine learning and automation.

Cybersecurity Lab

Advanced network simulation center focusing on ethical hacking, cryptography, and digital forensics.

Cloud Computing Lab

High-performance server environment for big data processing and cloud infrastructure management.
