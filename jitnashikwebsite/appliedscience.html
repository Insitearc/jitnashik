"The Applied Science and Humanities department is the backbone of engineering education, providing the fundamental principles that drive technological innovation."

The department plays a crucial role in building the foundation for all engineering disciplines. We focus on grooming First Year students through rigorous training in Mathematics, Physics, Chemistry, and Communication Skills.

Our goal is to transform young students into professional engineers by instilling a strong analytical mindset and robust ethical values from the very beginning of their academic journey.
