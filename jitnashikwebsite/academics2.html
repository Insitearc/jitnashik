Academic Governance

Jawahar Education Society's JIT Nashik is affiliated with **Savitribai Phule Pune University (SPPU)**, following a rigorous credit-based system that ensures global standards in engineering education.

SPPU Affiliation

Our curriculum is governed by SPPU, focusing on Outcome-Based Education (OBE). Students are evaluated through continuous internal assessments and university examinations.

Research & PBL

Project-Based Learning (PBL) is integrated into our pedagogy, encouraging students to apply engineering principles to solve real-world community problems.

Student Support Ecosystem

Beyond textbooks, we provide a structured support system including the Guardian Teacher Scheme, where every faculty member mentors a small group of students for their overall development.

Mentor Scheme

Individual monitoring of attendance, academic progress, and personal counseling.

Internships

Mandatory industrial training to bridge the gap between academia and industry requirements.

Pedagogical Highlights

Skill-Based Workshops

Regular certification courses in AI, IoT, and Cloud Computing conducted by industry experts.

Digital Learning

Subscription to NPTEL and remote access to e-journals through our digital resource center.
